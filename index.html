KeepSync

Self-hosted tab & bookmark sync across Chrome and Firefox. Your data stays on your server — no cloud, no tracking.

🦊 Get it on Firefox View on GitHub Privacy Policy

Features

🔒 Self-hosted

Your data lives on your own server. No third-party cloud, no tracking, no data mining.

🔄 Cross-browser

Sync tabs and bookmarks between Chrome and Firefox with the same self-hosted backend.

⚡ Real-time

WebSocket and SSE support with polling fallback for instant sync across devices.

📱 Device management

List, name, and revoke devices from the options page. Revocation propagates in real time.

🔖 Bookmark sync

Full bookmark tree sync with conflict resolution and UUID-based portable IDs.

📜 History backfill

Automatically backfill missed history events after a crash or offline period.

Open source & privacy-first

MIT licensed. Inspect the code, self-host the server, and control your own data.
